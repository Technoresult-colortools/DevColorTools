About AI Color Palette Generator

The AI Color Palette Generator allows you to create beautiful and harmonious color palettes based on a simple text prompt and theme selection. Utilizing advanced AI, this tool generates color schemes tailored to your specific needs, providing you with a versatile palette that can be used for web design, branding, and more.

How to Use It?

Enter a descriptive prompt that outlines your color inspiration, such as "sunset over the ocean" or "vintage autumn vibes." Then, choose a theme from the dropdown menu, such as "Light" or "Dark." Click the "Generate" button, and the AI will create a custom color palette for you. You can preview the palette on a sample design or export it for use in your projects.
